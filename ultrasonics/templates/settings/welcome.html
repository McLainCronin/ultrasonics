This is the homepage. It shows all the applets you've created, with general information on the left, and controls on the right of each.

When you create a new applet, you are greeted with the applet builder screen. Each applet contains some combination of:

  • Inputs (Where your playlists are coming from)
  • Modifiers (Make interesting alterations to your playlists)
  • Outputs (Where your playlists are going to)
  • Triggers (What causes the applet to run)

You need at least one input and one output for a functional applet!

All the plugins you can use to build you applet are shown at the plugin selection screen.

Some plugins require 'persistent settings'. These settings will carry over to all instances of the specific plugin.

Some plugins also have instance specific settings. These are specifically for this applet, and will control the plugins behaviour.
